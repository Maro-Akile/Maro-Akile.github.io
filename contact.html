Need help, want a commission, or just wanna chat?


Contact details are at bottom of this page. Read the texts below before contacting me.




I need help with project X. / I don't understand project X.

Summary: Read information guides first, then post on GitHub Issues or text me on Discord.

Right under the download links in my site, there is an info button where you can read the best documentation I could write for my individual projects. At the GitHub downloads, there is also a similar if not identical tutorial in the README.md. If you still need help, open an Issue on the project's GitHub page. If you would prefer a direct conversation with me, try contacting my Discord account listed near the bottom of this page.


There is a problem in project X. / I found a bug in project X.

Summary: Report bugs through the Google Form.

Check both the info page on this site for the project and the GitHub Issues page for the project. If the bug noted is already identical to yours, there is no more need to report it. If the bug is different or not documented, please report in the Google Form. If you would also like help on resolving the bug, post it on GitHub Issues after submitting the Google Form.


I want access to the WIPs. / I want to join the early testings.

Summary: Fill up the Registration Google Form.

To prevent stealing of my unfinished works, I only open WIP access to people I trust and people I know in real life. If you wish to join, please fill up the Google Form at the bottom of this page. If you are a stranger, I will likely not accept your application. I recommend interacting with me online (preferably through Tumblr) for at least a month, so that I will not consider you a total stranger. I hope you do not take my caution as a personal issue against you.


I want to contribute to project X.

Summary: Fill up the Registration Google Form.

I value my projects very much, they are like children to me. So, I will not easily let other people work on them. However, if I consider you as someone I can trust or I like your vibes, I may let you. If you want to contribute, fill up the Google Form and I will let you know if you will get access to the project of your choice.


I have a suggestion.

Summary: Put your suggestions in the Suggestion Google Form.

I made a Google Form for suggestions. The Suggestions form is not limited to adding new features, but also for improving this site and my projects. You can be honest, but please be nice in the Suggestions form!


I want a commission.

Summary: Place your order in the Commissions Google Form.

You can order a commission through the Google Form. However, since I currently don't have ways of exchanging money online, I can only receive money from people I know in real life. If you still want a commission, we could probably arrange a way to trade online. (ex. your art for my code)


I just want to chat.

Summary: Try my Discord Server.

I made a Discord Server just for that! Occasionally, I will be online to chat with you. If I'm not online, you can chat with the other people in the server.
